# Gym Growth Engine

**Pack id:** gym-growth-engine
**Name:** Gym Growth Engine
**One-liner:** The operator's stack for filling, selling, and scaling a gym using the offer, lead-gen, and money-model frameworks behind Gym Launch and Acquisition.com.
**Version:** 1.0.0

Twelve skills that operate a gym business rather than explain theory. Each one
re-expresses a published framework in operational form: a step, a runnable
calculator, or a fill-in template. Examples run on one realistic gym, a single
location selling memberships and semi-private coaching with a 6-week
transformation challenge as the front-end.

This document is the human-readable manifest. In Skill Me the pack is defined in
`lib/pack-definitions.ts` and the 12 skills live in the catalog, exported to
`skills/<slug>/SKILL.md`.

## Modules and skills

### Module A. Offer and Pricing

- **grand-slam-offer-builder**: Build an offer the right prospect feels stupid
  saying no to with the Value Equation and the offer stack. Reach for it when the
  offer is not converting or needs a name.
- **gym-pricing-and-guarantees**: Set premium price points and design guarantees
  that remove buyer risk without bleeding margin. Reach for it when setting or
  raising prices or deciding whether a guarantee is safe.

### Module B. Lead Generation

- **core-four-lead-engine**: Plan and run the four ways to get known: warm
  outreach, content, cold outreach, paid ads, with the daily volume math. Reach
  for it when you need more leads or a lead plan.
- **gym-transformation-challenge**: Design the 6-week challenge front-end that
  fills the gym and converts to membership. Reach for it when building or running
  a challenge.
- **gym-meta-ads-funnel**: Write, structure, and budget the Meta ad funnel that
  fills the challenge. Reach for it for ad copy, budgets, or when cost per lead is
  too high.
- **referral-and-affiliate-system**: Get others to sell for you with the four
  lead-getters: referrals, affiliates, agencies, employees. Reach for it to build
  referrals or local partnerships.

### Module C. Sales and Retention

- **closer-sales-script**: Run the intro consult to close with the CLOSER
  framework and a word-for-word gym script. Reach for it to close more consults.
- **objection-handling-and-speed-to-lead**: Contact leads within minutes and
  dissolve the four core objections. Reach for it when leads go cold or consults
  stall on price, time, spouse, or "let me think about it."
- **retention-and-churn-killer**: Engineer onboarding, results, and the first
  100 days to cut churn. Reach for it when members quit or retention is weak.

### Module D. Scale and Operations

- **gym-money-model**: Model CAC, 30-day cash, LTV, LTV:CAC, payback, and
  Client-Financed Acquisition. Reach for it to decide whether growth pays for
  itself. Install this first.
- **hiring-a-players-and-sops**: Recruit A-players with a role-outcome funnel and
  document work into transferable SOPs. Reach for it when hiring or getting out of
  the day-to-day.
- **kpi-scoreboard-and-cadence**: Build the scoreboard and the daily, weekly, and
  monthly cadence that runs the gym on numbers. Reach for it to track the right
  KPIs and find the weakest funnel stage.

## Recommended install sequence

1. **Know the math first:** gym-money-model.
2. **Build the offer:** grand-slam-offer-builder, then gym-pricing-and-guarantees.
3. **Fill it:** core-four-lead-engine to plan the channels, then
   gym-transformation-challenge and gym-meta-ads-funnel to execute.
4. **Sell it:** closer-sales-script, then objection-handling-and-speed-to-lead.
5. **Keep them:** retention-and-churn-killer.
6. **Scale it:** referral-and-affiliate-system, kpi-scoreboard-and-cadence, and
   hiring-a-players-and-sops.

The skills reference each other in this order. The money model feeds challenge
pricing, ad budgets, and the value of retention; the scoreboard reads the funnel
the lead and sales skills produce.

## Methodology and IP note

This pack encodes published business methods (the Value Equation, the Grand Slam
Offer, the Core Four, Client-Financed Acquisition, the CLOSER consult) in
operational form. It re-expresses each method in its own language with no
verbatim reproduction of book text. The name is function-first by design;
descriptions reference Hormozi, Gym Launch, and Acquisition.com only as the
methodology lineage. For a public listing, keep the function-first name.
